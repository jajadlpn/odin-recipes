Scrambled Eggs

A healthy meal for a healthy heart.

Shiouzy's Scrambled Eggs is incredibly easy to cook. Perfect as it is or with your choice of bread.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Crack eggs and pput in a bowl.
  2. Beat until the white and yellow parts of the egg are well mixed.
  3. Pour a bit of milk into the mix.
  4. Pour the mix into a pan, mixing it occasionally.
  5. Don't overcook egg. Once it's starting to cook, mash it a little.
  6. Serve with bread.
