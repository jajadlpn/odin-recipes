Buttered Shrimp

A delicious blend of seafood and dairy.

Shiouzy's Buttered Shrimp is a classic. Incredibly delicious escpecially when the you cook the shrimp just right.

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Heat up pan with butter.
  2. Put shrimp in pan and stir every few minutes.
  3. Wait until shrimp turns a light orange
  4. Do not overcook shrimp, it will make the skin hard to peel.
