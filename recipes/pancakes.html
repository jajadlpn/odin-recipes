Pancakes

A quick recipe to fuel up your morning.

Shiouzy's Pancake recipe is perfect for on-the-go people. It takes less than 10 minutes and gives you enough fuel to conquer the morning.

Ingredients:

  • Pancake Mix
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Maple Syrup

Steps:

  1. Put pancake mix in a bowl.
  2. Pour milk into the mix.
  3. Mix to create a batter.
  4. Add chocolate chips to the batter.
  5. Pour just enough batter in a pan with bytter.
